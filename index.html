Which products form part of the BIRKENSTOCK NATURAL SKIN CARE cosmetics range?

The product range of BIRKENSTOCK NATURAL SKIN CARE encompasses natural cosmetics products, developed for the targeted cleansing and care of the body and face. The range is rounded off by special products for hand and foot care.

How can I be sure that BIRKENSTOCK NATURAL SKIN CARE is also natural cosmetics?

All BIRKENSTOCK NATURAL SKIN CARE products are certified according to the BDIH COSMOS standard. The BDIH COSMOS seal of approval is an international standard for natural cosmetics that gives you confidence about the quality of ingredients and the manufacturing process of products. To qualify for this certification, a product has to meet the strict regulatory requirements on the selection of resources and the production processes.
